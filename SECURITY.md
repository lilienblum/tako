# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| 0.x.x   | :white_check_mark: |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Report any discovered vulnerabilities by emailing `security@tako.sh`. Your report will be acknowledged within 5 days, and a team member will be assigned as the primary handler. To the greatest extent possible, the security team will endeavor to keep you informed of the progress being made towards a fix and full announcement, and may ask for additional information or guidance surrounding the reported issue.
